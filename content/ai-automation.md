## 2. What Are AI Agents?
<!-- LAYOUT: explainer. Plain-language text left, simple agent-loop diagram right.
     Diagram: Input → Reason → Act (tools/APIs) → Check → repeat. SVG/CSS, ochre on paper. -->
<!-- EYEBROW (mono, ochre): "The concept" -->

An AI agent is software that can take a goal, break it into steps, use tools and
APIs to act, and check its own work — instead of waiting for a human at every step.

Used well, that means routine, multi-step tasks get done reliably in the background.
Used carelessly, it's a liability. The difference is architecture and guardrails.
